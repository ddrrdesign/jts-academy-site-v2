FIRST JTS Academy is an educational robotics platform built by an active FIRST team. We help students grow through real competition experience, teamwork, and engineering thinking.

About FIRST JTS Academy

FIRST JTS Academy is a robotics and STEM education platform for students aged 6–18. We work with FIRST programs and focus on real competition experience, teamwork, and engineering thinking.

Who is it for

Students aged 6–18 interested in robotics and technology.

Our academy is designed for motivated students who want more than theory. We help them grow from basic robotics to real competition-level engineering.

How we teach

Hands-on learning through projects and teamwork.

Students design, build, test, fail, improve, and learn how engineering works in practice - not just in theory.

Competition focus

Preparation for official FIRST competitions.

We train teams for regional and international competitions, including strategy, robot design, documentation, teamwork, and judging.
